Job Summary

We are seeking a thorough and dependable Housekeeper to maintain a clean, organized, and comfortable living or working environment. The ideal candidate will take pride in their work, and consistently uphold high standards of cleanliness and presentation.

  • Perform general housekeeping and laundry duties
  • Operate basic cleaning equipments efficiency

  • Adhere to health and safety regulations and company housekeeping/laundry standards
  • Follow housekeeping schedules and checklists for assigned areas
  • Report any maintenance issues or safety hazards to management
  • Comply with local laws and company policies
